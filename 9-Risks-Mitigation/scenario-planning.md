# Scenario Planning

- **Bull Case:** ADGM licence approved within three months, 1 000 paying users by month 12 and annual recurring revenue (ARR) of US $1.2 m.
- **Base Case:** White‑label partnership in place by month 3, 500 paying users by month 12 and ARR of US $0.6 m.
- **Bear Case:** Paper trading only for 12 months, 200 active users and pre‑revenue status. The team focuses on product improvements and compliance preparation.

Plans and budgets will be adjusted based on scenario outcomes and evolving market conditions.
