# Insurance & Legal Protections

ReFi.Trading will obtain the following insurance policies as the business scales:

- **Directors & Officers (D&O) insurance:** Protects directors and officers against claims arising from their management decisions.
- **Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance:** Covers professional liability for potential trading errors or system failures.
- **Cyber insurance:** Provides coverage for data breaches and cyber‑attacks.

In addition the company will implement indemnification agreements for directors and employees and maintain up‑to‑date legal documentation.
