# Use of Funds (Series A)

The Series A round will fund growth over the next 12 months:

- **40 % – Product & engineering:** Expand the team, develop Phase 2 and Phase 3 features, improve infrastructure.
- **25 % – Compliance & licensing:** Cover application fees, legal counsel and regulatory capital requirements in the UAE.
- **20 % – Growth & marketing:** Produce content, run influencer campaigns and attend industry events.
- **10 % – Operations:** Fund office expenses, insurance and administrative costs.
- **5 % – Contingency:** Reserve for unexpected expenses and risk mitigation.

These allocations are indicative and will be refined once fundraising is complete.
