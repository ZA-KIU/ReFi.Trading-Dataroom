# Market Trends and Tailwinds

- **Growth of retail trading:** More individuals are actively trading equities and digital assets, spurred by accessible broker apps and social media.
- **AI adoption in finance:** Reinforcement learning and machine learning models are increasingly being applied to trading, portfolio management and risk analysis.
- **Decentralisation movement:** Post‑FTX, there is heightened awareness of counter‑party risk and demand for non‑custodial solutions.
- **Regulatory maturation:** Jurisdictions such as the UAE and the EU are introducing frameworks that legitimise and encourage fintech innovation.
