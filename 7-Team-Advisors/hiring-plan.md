# Hiring Plan (12‑18 Months)

- **Month 0–3:** Hire a chief technology officer or fractional CTO to lead architecture. Recruit one ML engineer and one compliance officer.
- **Month 3–6:** Add a growth marketer and a customer support lead. Begin recruiting a finance officer for the ADGM entity.
- **Month 6–12:** Scale the engineering team with two additional ML engineers and a DevOps specialist. Hire a customer success manager to support paid users.
- **Month 12–18:** Expand the compliance team and consider hiring a chief risk officer as live trading volumes grow.
