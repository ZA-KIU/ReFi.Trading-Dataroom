# Licences & Approvals

- **UAE – ADGM Category 3A licence:** Target jurisdiction for the primary regulatory approval. Allows dealing and arranging investments without custody.
- **EU – MiCA / MIFID (Lithuania or Cyprus):** Secondary option for expansion into Europe. Pursued after ADGM approval.
- **US – RIA or broker partnership:** Long‑term target. The company may act as a Registered Investment Adviser or integrate through a partner broker.
- **Other jurisdictions:** Exploratory discussions in Singapore and Hong Kong for future phases.
