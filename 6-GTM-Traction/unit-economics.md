# Unit Economics

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Target under US $100 per weekly active safe paper trader.
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Estimated US $2 400 based on a US $100/month subscription over 24 months with 50 % retention.
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** 24× (industry benchmark for healthy SaaS businesses).
- **Payback Period:** 1 month, assuming high conversion from paper trading to paid subscriptions.
- **Gross Margin:** 85 %+ as the platform is primarily software‑based.
